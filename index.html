About me

Hi! I am Chloe. Located in the North Pole, I am a non-binary computer science student who likes to build software and translate projects around the Internet to Russian.

I often like to experiment with Node.js, TypeScript and SASS, but I'm comfortable with using JavaScript, CSS, HTML and PostgreSQL.

I'm also keen in tinkering with vintage computer hardware and transforming or upgrading older hardware into NAS/media servers or game servers entirely.
